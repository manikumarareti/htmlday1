PM Modi's 'mini Diwali' with 'Ram Jyoti' diyas at home after Ayodhya return

Prime Minister Narendra Modi on Monday celebrated 'mini Diwali' at his residence, following his arrival in Delhi from Ayodhya. The Prime Minister lit diyas (earthen lamps) at his residence and shared pictures on X, formerly Twitter, with the caption "Ram Jyoti".
Earlier today, PM Modi performed rituals of Ram Lalla's 'Pran Pratishtha' as 'mukhya yajman'.
While several people, including union ministers and other politicians, celebrated ‘Deepotsav’ at their homes, some also took to the streets and lit up special lights and burst firecrackers.

Ambanis donate Rs 2.51 crore to Ayodhya’s Ram Mandir Trust

Reliance Industries Limited chairman Mukesh Ambani and his family attended the Ram Mandir Pran Pratishtha ceremony in Ayodhya. The Ambani family announced a donation of Rs 2.51 crore to the temple trust.

Zomato customer flags unavailability of chicken. Food delivery platform replies

Zomato said they have ‘disabled delivery of non-veg items’ in certain states in accordance with government instructions.
Zomato on Monday responded to a customer's complaint on X regarding the unavailability of chicken for food delivery, saying that they have ‘disabled delivery of non-veg items’ in certain states in accordance with government instructions.
The user tagged the food-delivery app and humorously mentioned, “Zomato is not delivering chicken in bhopal today lmao”. Speculating about the reason, the user added, "“tbh it could be because of supply of meat not being available today v/s anything decision by zomato.”
Zomato replied, clarifying,“Hi, we have disabled delivery of non-veg items in Uttar Pradesh, Assam, ⁠Chhattisgarh, Madhya Pradesh and Rajasthan as per govt. notice. Hope this clarification helps!”
This decision aligns with Uttar Pradesh's, along with a few other states', move to ban the sale of meat and fish on Monday, coinciding with the consecration ceremony of the Ram Temple in Ayodhya.
In a grand ceremony, Prime Minister Narendra Modi participated in the consecration ceremony of Lord Ram Lalla in Ayodhya on Monday, with several celebrities and business leaders attending the event.
